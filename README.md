# Drivewise: Road to Responsibility 🚦

## Overview

**Drivewise: Road to Responsibility** is a Java-based text adventure game designed to promote **road safety awareness and responsible transportation practices in the Philippines**.

The game places the player in real-life traffic situations where they must make decisions based on **traffic signs, road rules, and safety practices**. Through challenges and scenarios, players learn how responsible driving behavior contributes to safer roads and better transportation systems.

## Game Concept

In Drivewise, the player takes the role of a driver navigating different transportation scenarios. Each situation presents **choices and questions** related to traffic rules, road signs, passenger interaction, and vehicle safety.

Correct decisions reward the player with **experience points (XP) and badges**, while incorrect choices demonstrate the consequences of unsafe driving practices.

## Features

* 🚗 Interactive **text-based adventure gameplay**
* 🛑 Challenges involving **traffic signs and road markings**
* 📜 Scenarios based on **Philippine traffic rules and regulations**
* ⭐ **XP and badge system** for player progression
* 🎮 Decision-making gameplay that simulates real-world driving situations
* 📖 Story-driven chapters that guide the player through different learning experiences

## Gameplay Structure

The game is divided into several chapters that gradually introduce different transportation concepts:

* **Chapter 1:** Introduction to driving responsibility
* **Chapter 2:** Traffic signs and road markings
* **Chapter 3:** Vehicle safety management
* **Chapter 4:** Traffic rules and regulations
* **Chapter 5:** Passenger interaction and road ethics
* **Chapter 6:** Advanced driving decisions and final challenges

Each chapter includes **multiple-choice questions, decision points, and scenario-based challenges**.

## Technologies Used

## Project Purpose

This project was developed as an educational tool to demonstrate how **interactive software and gamification can help teach transportation safety concepts**. It aims to raise awareness of responsible driving and highlight the importance of understanding traffic systems.
